About site

The site describes a board game Ludo. A very famous game and could be found usually in every home and mobiles nowadays.
Back in India it has been a part of my entire childhood and I have been playing the game since ever.
When given the opportunity to make a site about non-electronic game, Ludo came to my mind.
So here is my site which explains Ludo and hope you may become part of the entire community who loves to play Ludo.
