Reason to choose BTS:

As an Army (part of BTS fandom), when I saw the opportunity to write about my favorite artist, I chose BTS.
BTS has been a part of my life since past three years. Their songs motivate me to do better things and to see a better version of myself.
Its not only the songs that I love but I respect the members. The members from time to time reminds us to love ourselves.
They said: True love first begins with loving myself
With BTS everyday is a new chapter with an old lesson to love myself and see beauty of the world.
Hence, it was my honour to write about BTS.
